Applications

Illuminate is a deep engine with the ability to power better choices and interactions for a virtuous chain of users.

Below is a small collection of what devices or systems we believe can be powered by Illuminate.

    Season

    Connecting retailers to what’s fresh and local today.

    Season is an online networking platform that connects local producers to commercial retailers. We make it easy for big box stores to source the fresh & seasonal products today’s health conscious customer desires.

    ReRoute

    Catch it when it counts.

    ReRoute directs fresh food towards bellies and away from landfills. Re-route backstage automatically directs food food towards store locations where it can be sold at the peak of freshness - streamlining the supply chain and maximizing profit. Our center-stage app offers surge-pricing coupons to get you to the produce you need.

    This is YOUR apple.

    Fillet

    Complete transparency about your food, right in your grocery aisle.

    Never doubt where your meats and fish come from, how fresh they are, or if they're going to give you a bad case of salomonella. Spectroscopy helps you trace the origins and legitimacy of your food, and allows you to delve into every level of information you'd want to know.

    Nutriceipt

    Never doubt if you're getting enough vitamins and minerals, ever again.

    We're all familiar with the concept of grocery stores giving you a receipt for how much money you spent at the end of your trip, but what does the numerical value actual hold for you? Nutriceipt gives you a read out of the nutritional content of everything you've bought, giving you a better sense of control of your health.
