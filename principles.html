Design Principles

These guiding principles informed all of our prototyping and concepts. They are built from the insights gathered from user and expert interviews.

1. Open Mic
2. Nature Knows Best

4. Help Me Choose

Open Mic

When it comes to food - people have stories to tell. Illuminate provides the facts to back it up. Our platform enable small, medium, and large scale vendors to verify their value, and tell their own stories. A better food system is built on solid partnerships and relationships, and lots of them. Illuminate has power when it sits across and outside of the entire supply chain.

Nature Knows Best

In today’s food system, price sets the bottom line. Tomorrow’s food system will value quality - the way nature intended. Technology and real-time data mapping can allow us to tune into the rythms of seasonality, and re-shape our markets and standards according to natural constraints - and reduce waste.

Tell it Straight

Illuminate allows us to cut through all the jargon and shady games of today’s food system. We take a back to basics approach, giving you nothing but the truth you deserve.

Help Me Choose

Data is nothing if it doesn’t inform decision. Back of house teams crave tools that help determine actionable steps, or streamline complicated processes. Individual consumers want to know personalized, recommended food options.
