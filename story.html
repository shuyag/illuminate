Dave

As a fisherman, Dave saw an opportunity in the market to help other local fisherman. He create a company that incorporated storytelling and technology to match consumers with their local fisherman. By representing 1,000 fisherman, he has enough fish to have endless supply but is having a hard time finding the matching demand. He believes part of the problem is consumers want certain fish year round and lack the knowledge of seasonality.

''The industry is menu driven...I wish it was ocean driven.''

Mary

Mary is a local beekeeper and owner of Follow the Honey, a retail space for global honey and bee-inspired designs. Beyond the scope of sales, Mary has a larger mission to support human rights through her sourcing, and is developing a 3rd party certification for Honey that supports Human Rights. She wants be able to ensure the purity of her product, and that profits are benefiting the women beekeepers at the end of her supply chain.

''People should get rewarded for doing the right thing. You can't tell half the story and rewarded all the same...customers will pay more once they hear the story of how the honey was made. ''

Target Guests

Over a three day test in target stores, we had around 120 conversations with guests around the Ten scale, Dynamic Pricing and Good & Gather. We observed that purchases were primarily motivated by price. Consumers expressed interest in learning more about freshness, food waste, and the meaning of natural, but were most excited for technology that could provide actionable steps & personalized information.

''I want to know about freshness...how long are these bananas going to last? ''
''I want to know that this avocado is good for me.''
